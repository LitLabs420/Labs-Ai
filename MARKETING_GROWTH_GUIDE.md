- [ ] Revenue this week
- [ ] Most effective channel (Google? Facebook? Organic?)

### Monthly (1 hour):
- [ ] MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue)
- [ ] Customer LTV (average customer value √ó lifetime)
- [ ] CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost)
- [ ] LTV:CAC ratio (should be 3:1 or higher)
- [ ] Churn rate (% who canceled)
- [ ] Best performing ads/channels

### Formulas:
```
Conversion Rate = Signups / Visitors √ó 100
CAC = Ad Spend / New Customers
LTV = (Monthly Revenue / Customers) √ó 12
LTV:CAC = LTV / CAC (Target: 3:1+)
Churn = (Canceled / Starting Customers) √ó 100
```
