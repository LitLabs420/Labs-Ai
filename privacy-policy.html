1. Introduction & Overview

FLIPFORGE™ ("Company", "we", "us", or "our") operates the FLIPFORGE™ platform (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our website and services.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By accessing or using FLIPFORGE™, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by all the provisions of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our Service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Registration: Email address, password, name, company name, phone number, payment information
  • Billing & Payment: Credit card details, billing address, transaction history (processed securely via Stripe/PayPal)
  • Profile & Settings: Business type, website URL, social media handles, preferences, profile photo
  • Content You Create: Sales funnels, email templates, landing pages, chatbot configurations, customer data you upload
  • Communications: Support requests, feedback, chat messages, email inquiries
  • Analytics Data: Funnel views, conversion data, customer information you track within funnels

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, browser type, IP address, device identifiers
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, features used, clicks, interactions, session duration
  • Location Data: Approximate location based on IP address (not precise GPS)
  • Cookies & Tracking: Session cookies, analytics pixels, tracking IDs
  • Log Data: Server logs, error reports, access timestamps

2.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Payment Processors: Stripe, PayPal (payment verification, fraud prevention)
  • Social Media: Google OAuth, Facebook Login (if you authorize)
  • Email Services: Email deliverability data, bounce rates
  • Customer Data: Customer lists you upload (we store but don't use for our marketing)

4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

4.1 We Share Data With:

  • Stripe & PayPal: Payment processing (only payment details, not your full profile)
  • Firebase (Google Cloud): Infrastructure hosting, authentication, database management
  • Nodemailer & SendGrid: Email delivery (we don't share your email list)
  • Google Analytics: Anonymized usage analytics (no personally identifiable info)
  • Cloudflare: CDN and DDoS protection
  • Support Tools: Zendesk or similar (only tickets you create)

4.2 We Do NOT Share:

  • Personal data with advertisers or data brokers
  • Customer email lists or data you upload
  • Payment information beyond what processors need
  • Data for any purpose other than providing the Service (without your consent)

4.3 Legal Disclosures

We may disclose your information if required by law or legal process (court orders, subpoenas, government requests). We will attempt to notify you before disclosure unless legally prohibited.

5. Data Security

5.1 How We Protect Your Data

  • Encryption: TLS/SSL encryption for all data in transit; AES-256 for sensitive data at rest
  • Authentication: Firebase Auth with JWT tokens and session management
  • SHIELD AI System: Real-time fraud detection and anomaly monitoring
  • Access Controls: Role-based access (only admins access sensitive data)
  • Regular Audits: Security scanning and penetration testing
  • Backup & Recovery: Daily encrypted backups with disaster recovery procedures
Note: While we implement industry-standard security measures, no system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data. Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication for additional protection.
