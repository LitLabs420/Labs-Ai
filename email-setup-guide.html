Setup Options

Option 1: Google Workspace (Recommended) ‚≠ê

Setup Instructions:

  1. Go to Google Workspace (workspace.google.com)
  2. Sign up with your domain or use a forwarding domain
  3. Create user accounts for each email address above
  4. Cost: ~$6-12/user/month
  5. Includes: Gmail, Drive, Docs, Calendar

Why this is best: Professional, reliable, integrates with everything

Option 2: Email Forwarding (Budget)

Setup Instructions:

  1. Use your domain registrar's email forwarding
  2. Forward all emails to your personal email
  3. Cost: Usually FREE with domain
  4. Send emails "From:" the business address

Note: Emails appear to come from business address, but land in your personal inbox

Option 3: Amazon SES (High Volume)

Setup Instructions:

  1. Go to AWS SES (Simple Email Service)
  2. Verify your domain
  3. Cost: $0.10 per 1,000 emails
  4. Already integrated with Firebase Functions

Note: Best if you're sending hundreds of emails/month
